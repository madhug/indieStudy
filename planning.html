How did writing the Business Plan help you?
Dreamflight Adventures

Dream Filght Adventures, writing a version of the business plan showed that if it was a separate venture, the cost of rent and labor was very high to make it profitable. Unlike a movie theatre and arcades, the throughput of the experience is very low - 6 per showing/hour whereas in a movie theatre, it is over 200 per showing/hour. Knowing these issues, the entrepreneur can either choose to ignore it and go ahead with the exact same idea or choose not to work on this at all. However, the better option is to come up with creative ways of resolving these issues. In case of Dream Flight Adventures, the creative way of handling this was to work with schools, by increasing the educational content of the experience. Schools provided the space to setup the simulator and have staff at hand to help run the simulator.
